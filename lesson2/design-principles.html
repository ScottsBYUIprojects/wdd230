Visual Hierarchy

Alpha Lion

Alphalion.com

Alpha Lion exemplifies visual hierarchy as it is promoting a BOGO sale of a fat burning supplement, the eye catcher is the image of fire as looking at it almost makes you sweat, followed by three different flavored bottles of “super human scorch” and 2 tags above the bottles saying ‘2 days only” and “BOGO 50% OFF”, then the call to action says “SHOP NOW”, followed by a banner/notification above the header that repeats the 2 tags and CTA in a smaller font.

Fitt's Law

Idaho Power

Idahopower.com

Idaho power exemplifies the use of Fitt’s Law by providing 4 easy to find and reach buttons that are reasonably proportional to their expected frequency of use.

White space and clean design

Pest Share

Pestshare.com

Pest Share exemplifies the use of white space and clean design as you scroll down the page has plenty of white space, the text is minimal, and not too many pictures are used, all of this is promoting the idea of a service that provides a clean, sanitary, bug-free space in the home.
