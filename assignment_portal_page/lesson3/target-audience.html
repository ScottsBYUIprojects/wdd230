Description of the target audience

Residents, tourist and potential new residents of Waterdeep Large and small local and non-local businesses Food and service vendors

Personas

Persona: Long term member of the pest control industry at the ground level Fictional Name: Jim Hardy Job Title: Pest Management Professional, provides the highest level of face to face customer service, while key in promotion of the business he represents. Demographic: 32 years old, Married, father of 2, holds multiple applicator licenses. Goals and task: has 'lived and learned' and now spends his free time trying to get out of the rat race As his family grows, he is more conscious about how he spends and manages his time and money. Environment: He is 'tech savvy' uses his Iphone and Computer regularly for his communication, service reporting, side work, and school.

Persona: Real estate investor on his 10th single family rental home looking for his next deal. Fictional Name: Robert Bode Job Title: Recently retired from 25 years as a construction engineer in the private sector and now only manages his personal portfolio. Demographic: 55 years old, Married, father of 4, grandfather of 3, has a bachelors degree in science. Goals and tasks: He is swamped with managing his portfolio and wants to be able to focus on just finding deals. Spends his time taking maintenance related calls sending out cheap vendors that do 'a good job', doing property inspections, leasing, and sometimes property rehab. looking for ways to simplify the management process. Environment: still uses a flip phone, but has a desktop that a lot of his work is done on, but essentially lives under a rock.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: The majority of traffic will be by residents browsing for services available at reduced cost. For instance a resident with an appliance issue might have called around and received high estimates so they decided to look at the Chamber of Resident Benefits for an trusted reduced cost vendor.

Scenario 2: non-local businesses doing market research

Scenario 3: Someone who wants to be involved with the community looking for ways to get to know people can find a calendar of local events that might interest them.

Scenario 4: The chamber can also serve as a place to find trusted local vendors for those who want to support local businesses

Scenario 5: A member has questions or concerns about their membership and needs to find contact info
